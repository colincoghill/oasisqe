Examples
========

In this chapter, we walk through the installation and configuration of two common
scenarios. Use these to gain a better understanding of how to configure OASIS,
and also for ideas on how to implement your own setup.

OASIS is very configurable and can be customized for many situations. The downside
is that can also take some time to understand *how* to configure it in any given
situation.
