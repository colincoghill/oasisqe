Group Type

The type of a group sets the kind of activity the group is created for:

Staff
A collection of staff or assistants who can be assigned activities like course co-ordination or question creation.
Enrolment
A collection of students - can be assigned access to course assessments and practice.
Statistical
Grouping is used for statistical analysis and reporting. Members cannot see if they are in these groups.

Source

Where does the information on group membership come from?

Ad-Hoc
Maintained manually - members can be added or removed by staff.
External Feed
Membership information comes from an external source, eg. an LDAP server.
Open Registration
Users can enrol themselves into the group.
